Vicky Kaushal

Biography

Vicky Kaushal is an Indian actor who primarily works in Hindi films. He was born on May 16, 1988, in Mumbai, India. He made his acting debut in the film "Masaan" in 2015, which earned him critical acclaim and several awards. Since then, he has appeared in various successful films such as "Raazi," "Sanju," "Uri: The Surgical Strike," and "Bhoot Part One: The Haunted Ship."

Awards and Recognitions

  • 2019 - National Film Award for Best Actor for "Uri: The Surgical Strike"
  • 2019 - Filmfare Award for Best Actor for "Uri: The Surgical Strike"
  • 2020 - IIFA Award for Best Actor for "Uri: The Surgical Strike"
  • 2021 - Zee Cine Award for Best Actor (Male) for "Bhoot Part One: The Haunted Ship"
